1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website downloads onto your computer or other internet-enabled devices (such as mobile phones and tablets) when you visit a website. The cookie will help the website recognise your device the next time you visit.

Cookies do lots of different jobs, like letting you navigate between pages efficiently, remembering your preferences, and generally improving the user experience. Cookies may tell us, for example, whether you have visited the website before or whether you are a new visitor. They can also help to ensure that adverts you see online are more relevant to you and your interests.

Most cookies usually will not collect information that identifies you. Instead, they will collect more general information such as your general location or how you arrive at and use our websites.

2. Different types of cookies

The website uses the cookies that perform four functions, as classified below:

Category Description
Strictly Necessary cookies These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the website, enabling core functionalities such as site navigation and secure access. Without these cookies, services you have asked for cannot be provided.
First party analytics cookies These cookies facilitate data analysis to measure and enhance website performance while ensuring a more relevant user experience. They do not collect personally identifiable information at an individual level. In limited cases, data may be processed by authorized service providers acting on our behalf.
Performance cookies Primarily set by third-party vendors working with us, these cookies collect insights into how visitors interact with the website, such as frequently visited pages and error occurrences. The data gathered is anonymized and used solely to enhance website functionality.
Functional cookies These cookies enable the website to remember user preferences, such as language selection, region, or login details, to provide a more personalized experience. They do not track browsing activity outside this website or collect advertising data.
Advertising, Targeting and social media cookies These cookies help deliver personalized advertisements and optimize marketing strategies, including controlling ad frequency, measuring campaign effectiveness, and enabling retargeting across platforms.

3. Third-Party Cookies

These cookies are set by third parties when you access or use our website and have a different domain to that of the website you are visiting. They enable certain website functionalities, such as analytics and personalized content delivery.

Persistent Cookies: Do not expire at the end of each session but only at the expiry of the retention period or when deleted by the user.
Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when the browser closes. Used to track usage, register statistical data, and detect errors.

4. Cookie Consent

When you visit our website, a banner informs you about cookie usage and provides a direct link to this policy. You can “Accept” or “Reject All” cookies. However, essential cookies will remain active to provide basic website functions.

You can modify your cookie preferences anytime via the “Cookie Settings” at the bottom of each page or through your browser settings. Refusing cookies may affect website functionality.

5. How can you block cookies?

You can control whether to accept cookies or not. If you decide not to accept cookies, some features and services on our websites may not function properly. You can either:

  • Change your browser settings to notify you when you receive a cookie, and choose whether to accept it.
  • Set your browser to automatically not accept any cookies.

For more information on how we handle personal information please refer to our Privacy Notice.

6. Cookies that have been set in the past

If you have disabled one or more cookies, we may still use information collected prior to disabling. However, we will stop using the disabled cookie for future data collection.

7. Changes to this cookie policy

We may occasionally update this cookie policy. When changes are made, the “Last updated” date will be revised. If significant updates occur, a notice will be posted prominently on our website.
